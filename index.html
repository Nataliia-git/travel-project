Why Mallorca?

Mallorca - the largest island in all of Spain

The name of the island comes from an old Latin phrase 'insula maior', which means 'larger island', as Mallorca is the largest island in all of Spain. Gradually, due to the phrase mispronunciation, the island became known as Mallorca.
